Orders & Payment

We accept a variety of payment methods including: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and direct bank transfers. All payments are processed securely with SSL encryption and PCI DSS compliance.

Shipping & Delivery

Delivery times vary by location: Jordan: 1-3 business days. Middle East: 3-7 business days. International: 7-14 business days. Express shipping options are available at checkout for faster delivery.

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50. Orders under $50 have a flat shipping rate of $5.99 for domestic and $12.99 for international deliveries. During special promotions, we may offer free shipping on all orders regardless of amount.

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the buyer. Shipping costs and estimated delivery times for international orders are calculated at checkout.

Returns & Warranty

We offer a 30-day return policy on all products. Items must be returned in their original condition with all packaging and accessories. Simply contact our support team, receive an RMA number, and we'll arrange a free pickup. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. For full details, visit our Return Policy page.

All products come with the manufacturer's warranty plus a VoltStore 2-year standard warranty. We also offer VoltCare protection plans (Basic at $49/yr, Pro at $99/yr, Premium at $179/yr) for extended coverage including accidental damage protection. Visit our Services page for details.

If you receive a damaged or defective product, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage. We will immediately arrange either a free replacement or a full refund at no cost to you. Your satisfaction is our top priority.
