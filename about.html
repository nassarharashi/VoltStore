Our Story

From Passion to Innovation

Founded in 2018, VoltStore started as a small online shop with a big dream — to make premium electronics accessible to everyone. What began in a small office in Amman, Jordan, has grown into a trusted destination for tech enthusiasts across the region.

Our founders, passionate tech lovers themselves, noticed a gap in the market for a reliable, customer-first electronics retailer. They set out to build a store that combines the best product selection with exceptional customer service.

Today, VoltStore serves over 50,000 happy customers, offering 500+ products from the world's leading technology brands. We continue to innovate and expand, always putting our customers first.

Our Vision

To become the leading electronics retailer in the Middle East, recognized for unparalleled quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We envision a world where cutting-edge technology is accessible to everyone, empowering people to live smarter and more connected lives.

Our Mission

To deliver premium, authentic electronics at competitive prices while providing an exceptional shopping experience. We are committed to building lasting relationships with our customers through trust, transparency, and top-tier support at every step of their journey.
