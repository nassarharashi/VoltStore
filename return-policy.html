At VoltStore, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, our return policy makes it easy to return or exchange products.

1. Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return, the following conditions must be met:

  • The item must be returned within 30 days of the delivery date.
  • The product must be in its original condition, unused, and undamaged.
  • All original packaging, accessories, manuals, and tags must be included.
  • A valid receipt or proof of purchase is required.

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned or exchanged:

  • Software licenses and digital download products once activated.
  • Opened consumable items (batteries, ink cartridges, etc.).
  • Custom-built or personalized products.
  • Items with removed or altered serial numbers.
  • Products damaged due to misuse, negligence, or unauthorized modifications.

3. How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, follow these simple steps:

  • Step 1: Contact our support team at returns@voltstore.com or call +962 79 123 4567.
  • Step 2: Provide your order number and reason for the return.
  • Step 3: Receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number.
  • Step 4: Pack the item securely with the RMA number displayed on the package.
  • Step 5: Schedule a free pickup or drop off at our nearest location.

4. Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your return:

  • We will notify you via email about the approval or rejection of your refund.
  • Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
  • Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

5. Exchanges

Want a different product instead of a refund? We offer easy exchanges:

  • Exchanges are available for products of equal or greater value.
  • If exchanging for a higher-priced item, you'll pay the difference.
  • Exchange shipping is free of charge.

6. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective product:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Include photos of the damage and the packaging.
  • We will arrange an immediate replacement or full refund at no cost to you.

7. Contact Us

For return-related inquiries:

  • Email: returns@voltstore.com
  • Phone: +962 79 123 4567
  • Live Chat: Available 24/7 on our website
